FAQ

Questions people ask us most

Short answers, no judgement, and support at your pace.

Is the helpline confidential?

Yes. Conversations are private and anonymous. We never share your information.

What topics can I ask about?

Periods, pregnancy, contraception, abortion, and any questions you feel unsure about.

Do I have to decide right away?

No. We help you understand options and support your decision at your pace.

Is this a judgment-free space?

Always. Our counsellors are trained to listen, support, and never judge.

How do I reach a counsellor?

Call or schedule a call. We are here when you are ready.

Sahi Helpline connects you to trained experts for periods, pregnancy, and safe abortion — privately. The helpline connects callers to trained counsellors who listen carefully, share medically accurate information, and help individuals understand their options based on their unique situations. There is no pressure, no obligation, and no one-size-fits-all advice.

Conversations are kept completely confidential, and no personal information is collected or stored through the website. Sahi Helpline exists to ensure that when decisions are personal and sensitive, the information and support available are trustworthy, calm, and human.
